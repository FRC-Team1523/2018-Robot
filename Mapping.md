# Mappings

## Joystick

* Button 3 - Down shift
* Button 4 - Up shift
* Main trigger - Winch in / up

## Gamepad

* Analog Bumpers
    * Right - Arm Up
    * Left - Arm Down
* Bumpers
    * Left - Winch out / down
* Letters
    * X - Open grabbers
    * Y - Close grabbers
    * B - Box intake
    * A - Winch lock
